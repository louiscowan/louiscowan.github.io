Ethical situation

A situation where I have had to make an ethical decision was in my semi finals game of football. It was my last season of football playing for college and we were drawing 1-1. I ran down the field to the end of their half and got tackled. The ball went out for a throw in and the referee gave it to us. This was when I had to make an ethical decision as I was actually the last one to touch the ball, so it should really have been their throw in. I had to choose whether to not say anything and have a very good opportunity on goal or tell the referee that It was their throw in. I decided to tell the referee that I touched the ball last and it should really be their throw in. I have been taught my whole life that telling the truth, even when it doesn't work out for you, can be the best thing to do. I believe that both teams should tell the truth when it comes to things like this so that we may have a fair game. If I were in this situation again I would hope that I would do the same thing as I believe that it was still the right thing to do.
